The S19 vaccine, derived from a virulent strain of Brucella abortus, is widely used for cattle vaccination, offering strong protection but requiring careful management due to its potential to induce positive diagnostic results. The RB51 strain, a rough strain of Brucella abortus, provides effective immunity with fewer diagnostic interferences. Both vaccines are crucial for controlling Brucellosis in livestock and reducing transmission to humans.</p></p>
