<p><p>The micro display market comprises several technologies, each suited for specific applications. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers high contrast and fast response times, ideal for wearables and augmented reality. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) provides high resolution and color accuracy, commonly used in projectors and head-mounted displays. DLP (Digital Light Processing) uses micro mirrors for efficient and bright projections, widely found in cinema and business projectors. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) features affordability and versatility, used in various consumer electronics.</p></p>
