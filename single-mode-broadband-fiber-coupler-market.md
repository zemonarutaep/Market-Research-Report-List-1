<p><p>The Single Mode Broadband Fiber Coupler market is categorized into various split ratios, namely 1x2, 1x4, 1x8, 1x16, and 1x32. Each type indicates the number of output ports relative to a single input. For instance, a 1x2 coupler splits a single input into two outputs, while a 1x4 divides it into four. This hierarchy continues, with 1x8 offering eight outputs, 1x16 providing sixteen, and 1x32 delivering thirty-two, catering to diverse network scaling and distribution needs in telecommunications.</p></p>
