<p><p>The EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance) anti-theft antenna market primarily features three types: Radio Frequency (RF), Acousto-Magnetic (AM), and Electromagnetic (EM) systems. RF systems operate using radio waves to detect tags, while AM systems utilize magnetic fields and employ resonant tags for enhanced detection. EM systems rely on electromagnetic waves to sense security tags. Each technology offers distinct advantages in terms of detection range, compatibility with various tags, and environmental considerations, catering to diverse retail security needs and preferences.</p></p>
