<p><ul><li>Surface-mounted</li><li>Flush-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED underwater pool lights market features primarily two types: surface-mounted and flush-mounted lights. Surface-mounted lights are installed on the pool's walls, providing a visible fixture that illuminates the water effectively without extensive modifications. In contrast, flush-mounted lights are embedded within the pool structure, offering a sleek, integrated look while minimizing protrusions. Both types enhance pool aesthetics and safety, but their installation methods cater to different preferences for visibility and design in aquatic environments.</p></p>
