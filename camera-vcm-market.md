<p><ul><li>"Ball Structure VCM"</li><li>"Shrapnel Structure VCM"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Camera VCM (Voice Coil Motor) market comprises two primary types: Ball Structure VCM and Shrapnel Structure VCM. Ball Structure VCM utilizes balls in its design to ensure smooth movement and precise focusing, making it suitable for high-end cameras. In contrast, Shrapnel Structure VCM employs a flexible, metal plate mechanism that offers a compact design, ideal for smartphones and smaller devices. Both types enhance optical performance, catering to varying device requirements and user preferences in the imaging sector.</p></p>
