<p><p>The air starters market primarily features two types: Vane Air Starters and Turbine Air Starters. Vane Air Starters use rotating vanes to create torque, providing reliable starting for engines in various industrial and aerospace applications. They are known for their simplicity and compact design. In contrast, Turbine Air Starters utilize a turbine to generate high-speed rotation, making them suitable for larger engines and more demanding environments. Both types offer advantages in reliability, efficiency, and reduced maintenance needs, catering to diverse operational requirements.</p></p>
