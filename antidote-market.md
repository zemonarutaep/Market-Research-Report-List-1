<p><ul><li>Chemical Antidotes</li><li>Physical Antidotes</li><li>Pharmacological Antidotes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antidote market comprises three key types: chemical, physical, and pharmacological antidotes. Chemical antidotes work by directly neutralizing toxins, thereby preventing or reversing their effects. Physical antidotes act through mechanisms such as adsorbing toxins or diluting them in the body. Pharmacological antidotes involve drugs that counteract the effects of toxic substances by blocking their action on biological receptors. Together, these antidote types play a crucial role in managing poisonings and overdoses, enhancing patient safety and health outcomes.</p></p>
