<p><strong>The Polystyrene Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell Foam</li><li>Closed Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polystyrene Box Market comprises two primary types: Open Cell Foam and Closed Cell Foam. Open Cell Foam features interconnected cells, allowing air and moisture to circulate, making it lightweight and suitable for insulation and packaging applications. In contrast, Closed Cell Foam has tightly packed cells that resist moisture and provide better structural integrity, offering enhanced insulation properties and durability. Both types cater to various industries, including food packaging, construction, and electronics, each with unique advantages based on their cellular structure.</p></p>
