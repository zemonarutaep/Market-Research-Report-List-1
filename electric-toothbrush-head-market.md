<p><strong>The Electric Toothbrush Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Linear</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric toothbrush head market can be categorized into two primary types: reciprocating linear and rotary motion. Reciprocating linear toothbrush heads feature bristles that move back and forth in a straight line, effectively targeting plaque between teeth and along the gum line. In contrast, rotary motion toothbrush heads rotate in a circular motion, providing comprehensive cleaning by reaching difficult areas and effectively removing debris. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to different preferences and oral hygiene needs among consumers.</p></p>
