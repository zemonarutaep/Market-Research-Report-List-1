<p><p>EHV switches, or Extra High Voltage switches, are crucial in managing electrical power transmission. The market can be categorized into AC switches and DC switches. AC switches handle alternating current, essential for most grid applications, providing reliable operation under varying load conditions. In contrast, DC switches are designed for direct current systems, often used in renewable energy and high-voltage direct current (HVDC) transmission. Both switch types play vital roles in enhancing efficiency and safety in high-voltage power networks.</p></p>
