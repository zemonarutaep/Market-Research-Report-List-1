<p><ul><li>Core-Type Three-Phase Transformer(Single Core,Two Cores)</li><li>Shell-Type Three-Phase Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-phase transformer market is primarily categorized into core-type and shell-type transformers. Core-type transformers have a single core or two cores, featuring windings around a central magnetic core, ensuring compactness and efficient magnetic flow. Shell-type transformers, on the other hand, encase the windings within a magnetic shell, providing enhanced protection and thermal stability. Each type serves specific applications, with core-type transformers favored for space-constrained setups and shell-type transformers offering improved durability and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
