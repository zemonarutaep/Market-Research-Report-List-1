<p><p>The photon-counting computed tomography (CT) equipment market is primarily categorized into three types: Cadmium Zinc Telluride (CZT), Cadmium Telluride (CdTe), and other detector technologies. CZT detectors are known for their high spatial resolution and energy discrimination capabilities, making them valuable for detailed imaging. CdTe offers similar advantages but is often more cost-effective. Other market types may include technologies like silicon-based detectors or scintillator-based systems, each with unique performance characteristics tailored to specific imaging applications.</p></p>
