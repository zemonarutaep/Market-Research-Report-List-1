<p><ul><li>Two-Piece Systems</li><li>One-Piece Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stoma or ostomy care market primarily features two main types: one-piece systems and two-piece systems. One-piece systems combine the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering convenience and ease of use, especially for those with limited dexterity. Two-piece systems consist of a separate pouch and skin barrier, allowing for easier pouch changes without disturbing the adhesive barrier, providing flexibility and comfort. Both systems cater to varying patient needs and preferences, influencing their adoption in ostomy care.</p></p>
