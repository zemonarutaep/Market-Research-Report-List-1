<p><strong>The DNA Microarray Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide DNA Microarrays (oDNA)</li><li>Complementary DNA Microarrays (cDNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA microarray chip market consists of primarily two types: oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) and complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA). Oligonucleotide microarrays utilize short, single-stranded DNA probes to detect specific genes, offering high specificity and the ability to analyze gene expression patterns effectively. In contrast, complementary DNA microarrays are constructed from cDNA synthesized from mRNA, enabling the study of gene expression by comparing levels of transcription. Both types are instrumental in genomic research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
