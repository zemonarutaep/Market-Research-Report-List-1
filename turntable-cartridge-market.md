<p><strong>The Turntable Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MM</li><li>MI</li><li>MC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turntable cartridge market features three main types: Moving Magnet (MM), Moving Iron (MI), and Moving Coil (MC). MM cartridges use a magnet attached to the stylus, offering a good balance of sound quality and affordability, making them popular among casual listeners. MI cartridges use a cantilever with an iron element for improved sensitivity and output. MC cartridges, on the other hand, utilize coils directly attached to the stylus for high fidelity and detail, appealing to audiophiles despite their higher cost.</p></p>
