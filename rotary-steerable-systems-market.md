<p><strong>The Rotary Steerable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push the Bit</li><li>Point the Bit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary Steerable Systems (RSS) in the oil and gas industry enhance drilling precision and efficiency. The market is mainly divided into two types: Push the Bit and Point the Bit systems. Push the Bit systems utilize downhole tools to apply force directly to the drill bit, allowing for better control over the wellbore direction. In contrast, Point the Bit systems steer the drill bit through mechanical adjustments at the surface, offering enhanced maneuverability and adaptability to varying geological conditions.</p></p>
