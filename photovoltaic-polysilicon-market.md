<p><p>The photovoltaic polysilicon market comprises four main types: granular, blocky, flaky, and powder. Granular polysilicon features small, round particles, enhancing uniformity in solar cell production. Blocky polysilicon consists of larger, cube-shaped chunks, benefiting diffusion efficiency. Flaky polysilicon comprises thin, flat pieces that optimize surface area for improved electrical performance. Powdered polysilicon, with its fine grain structure, provides excellent reactivity and processing flexibility. Each type serves specific applications in the solar industry, impacting efficiency and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
