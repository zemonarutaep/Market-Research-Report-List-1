CF (Carbon Fillers) enhances electrical properties in plastics and composites. SCF (Super Conductive Fillers) boasts superior conductivity levels, making it ideal for advanced electronic applications. XCF (Extreme Conductive Fillers) offers the highest conductivity, suitable for specialized uses like sensors and high-performance electronics. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries, reflecting the versatility of conductive carbon blacks.</p></p>
