<p><p>Cell isolation and cell separation are critical techniques used in biological research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications. Cell isolation refers to the process of separating a specific type of cell from a heterogeneous cell mixture. This is crucial for studying individual cell behaviors and properties. Cell separation, on the other hand, involves the physical or biological processes that enable the separation of cells based on characteristics such as size, density, or surface markers. Technologies employed in these processes include flow cytometry, magnetic-activated cell sorting, and gradient centrifugation.</p><p>The Cell Isolation and Cell Separation Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for advanced cell-based therapies, regenerative medicine, and personalized medicine approaches.
