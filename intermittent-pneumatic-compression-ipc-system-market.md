<p><p>The Intermittent Pneumatic Compression (IPC) System market is divided into two main types: IPC garments and IPC devices. IPC garments are wearable pieces, typically designed to wrap around limbs, that apply pressure to enhance circulation and prevent conditions like deep vein thrombosis. IPC devices include the machinery that powers these garments, delivering controlled air compression cycles. Together, these components are used in medical settings to improve venous blood flow and reduce swelling, benefiting patients with various circulatory issues.</p></p>
