<p><strong>The Power Transmission Lines and Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Tension</li><li>Extra High Tension</li><li>Ultra High Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power transmission lines and towers are categorized based on their voltage levels, which determine their capacity to transmit electricity over long distances. High Tension (HT) lines operate between 11 kV to 66 kV, commonly used for regional distribution. Extra High Tension (EHT) lines range from 66 kV to 220 kV, facilitating inter-state transmission. Ultra High Tension (UHT) lines, exceeding 220 kV, are essential for long-distance, bulk electricity transfer, minimizing losses and improving efficiency in the power grid.</p></p>
