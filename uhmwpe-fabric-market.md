<p><p>The UHMWPE fabric market is categorized into three main types: woven, knitted, and non-woven fabrics. Woven fabrics are created by interlacing fibers for strength and durability, making them ideal for applications like ballistic protection. Knitted fabrics, produced by interlocking loops, offer greater elasticity and comfort, suitable for wearables and protective garments. Non-woven fabrics, made by bonding fibers together, provide versatility and are often used in medical and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct end-use sectors, reflecting varied performance characteristics.</p></p>
