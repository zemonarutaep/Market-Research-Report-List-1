<p><p>Multimode fiber patch cords are categorized into several standards based on their core diameter and performance characteristics. OM1 cables, with a 62.5 micron core, are suitable for short distances and basic applications. OM2 cables, featuring a 50 micron core, support higher bandwidth and longer distances. OM3 cables are optimized for 850 nm laser applications with improved bandwidth, while OM4 cables enhance performance further, making them ideal for high-speed networks. Each type caters to specific bandwidth and distance requirements, shaping the market accordingly.</p></p>
