<p><strong>The Motion Capture Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inertial Sensors</li><li>Measurement Sensors</li><li>Other Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion capture sensors market is segmented into three main types: inertial sensors, measurement sensors, and other sensors. Inertial sensors utilize accelerometers and gyroscopes to track motion by measuring changes in velocity and orientation. Measurement sensors focus on translating physical movements into digital data, often using technologies like optical or magnetic systems. Other sensors encompass a range of additional technologies, including pressure sensors and ultrasonic sensors, which enhance motion capture capabilities by providing complementary data for more comprehensive analysis.</p></p>
