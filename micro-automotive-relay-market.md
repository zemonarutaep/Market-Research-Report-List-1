<p><p>The micro automotive relay market is primarily segmented into plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are designed for easy installation and replacement, typically used in automotive applications requiring quick access, such as lighting and power distribution. In contrast, PCB relays are mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, offering a compact design ideal for modern vehicles where space is limited. Both types serve critical roles in vehicle electrical systems, ensuring efficient operation and reliability.</p></p>
