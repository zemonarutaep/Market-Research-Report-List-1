<p><p>The car parking system market is categorized into three main types: Mechanical Systems, Semi-Automated Systems, and Automated Systems. Mechanical Systems utilize lift and conveyor technology to park cars, optimizing space. Semi-Automated Systems offer a mix of manual and automated processes, enhancing convenience while requiring some user involvement. Automated Systems fully automate parking, allowing vehicles to be parked and retrieved with minimal human interaction, maximizing efficiency and reducing labor costs. Each type addresses specific space, cost, and user experience needs.</p></p>
