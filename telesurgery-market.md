<p><p>Telesurgery is a form of minimally invasive surgery that utilizes advanced telecommunication technologies to allow surgeons to perform operations remotely. This innovative approach combines robotics, high-definition video, and secure data networks, enabling specialists to operate on patients located in different geographical locations. This growth is fueled by advancements in robotic surgery systems, increasing investments in healthcare technology, and rising consumer demand for telehealth services. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the adoption of remote healthcare solutions, highlighting the importance of telesurgery in maintaining surgical care delivery while minimizing the risk of infection.</p><p>As healthcare facilities continue to integrate digital solutions, the market is likely to see increased partnerships between technology providers and healthcare institutions.
